About First Class Solutions

Delivering Excellence in Business & Financial Consulting Since 2006

Our Story

Founded in 2006 by Dr. Kazeem Ogbara, First Class Solutions Nig Ltd. has grown into a trusted name in business analytics, investment advisory, and corporate strategy across Nigeria and beyond.

We specialize in helping businesses make data-driven decisions, optimize financial performance, and achieve sustainable growth through expert consulting and research-based insights. Our client portfolio spans SMEs, multinational firms, government agencies, and non-profit organizations.

Mission

To empower organizations with strategic insight, financial clarity, and operational excellence through innovative, client-focused solutions that drive measurable results.

Vision

To be the preferred consulting partner for businesses and institutions seeking world-class advisory services in Africa and globally.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We operate with honesty, transparency, and unwavering ethical standards.
  • Excellence: We deliver high-quality, impactful results through rigorous analysis and professionalism.
  • Innovation: We embrace cutting-edge data tools, research methodologies, and modern frameworks.
  • Client-Centricity: Your goals are our mission — we listen, adapt, and deliver tailored success.

Why Choose Us?

With over 18 years of experience, a team of certified consultants, and a proven track record of transforming businesses, First Class Solutions combines local expertise with global best practices to deliver exceptional value.
