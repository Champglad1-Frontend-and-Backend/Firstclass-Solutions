Dr. Kazeem Ogbara, PhD, MBA, BA, HCIB, FICA, FIMC, CMC

Founder & CEO | Business Analytics | Investment Advisor | Banking Expert

Professional Summary

Dr. Kazeem Ogbara is a globally recognized expert in Business Analytics, Investment Advisory, and Credit Management, with over 30 years of experience in commercial and investment banking.

He began his banking career at International Merchant Bank (IMB) Plc and rose through the ranks at Eko International Bank and Skye Bank Plc, where he was named Best Performing Business Development Manager (2007).

He later served as Acting Regional Head and Regional Director (Greater Lagos), managing top-performing branches and leading strategic growth.
